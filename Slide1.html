Remembering Abdul Kalam,one of the first leaders to talk about skill development

On APJ Abdul Kalam's birth anniversary,let us know a little more about his contribution towards encouraging youth in seeking skill development training.

The missile man,peoples president,great leader,scientist,teacher, and most importantly a good human being. These are the adjectives we use for India's 11th president Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam aka Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam.

Kalam has influenced many lives with his charismatic personality and curious mind,especially he has been an idol to many teachers and students.

APJ Abdul Kalam's Birth is celebrated as the World student's day because of his untiring contribution to the world of teching and he identified himself foremost in that role before anything else.

He was indeed one of the most successful and influential presidents of India,but also he has been an advocate of learning and education.His words are still being quoted by teachers and also students from day to day.

Kalam's expectation from Indian education

He had great expectations from the education sector of India,he believed it was the knowledge that made a person great.At an event held in 2015 at Delhi Secretariat,he said he claims to be a teacher since the beginning of his career.

He was one the leaders who talked about the inportance of Skill-based learning in school.He said students should have 2 certificates when they pass out of school one of the 10+2 passing certificate and the one of a specific skill acquired by them.
